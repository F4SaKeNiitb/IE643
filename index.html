How It Works

This application captures audio from your microphone in 5-second segments and sends them to our server for transcription. The enhanced model combines two state-of-the-art speech recognition systems to provide better accuracy, especially for challenging audio conditions.

Processing: Audio is recorded at 16 kHz and processed using GPU acceleration for fast results.

Context Window: The model uses the previous 15 seconds of audio as context to improve accuracy across segments.

Latency: Expect approximately 5-6 seconds between speaking and seeing your transcription.
